# Chapter: Spatterjay 1 - The Skinner

## Events
Keech arrives on Spatterjay, landing his scooter near a stone tower on a poisoned clearing. He communicates with Olian Tay via intercom, who scans him for weapons before allowing him entry. Inside, Keech questions Tay about the Spatterjay virus, her altered appearance (“going native”), and the fates of individuals like Aphed Rimsc and Hoop. Tay reveals Hoop may still be alive, possibly disguised as the “Skinner” on the Segre Islands, and suggests Keech seek out Old Captains for more information. Simultaneously, Ambel and his crew aboard the *Treader* navigate sargassum islands, hunting “prill” and encountering a glister carcass. Keech explores Tay’s house, discovering a museum filled with disturbing exhibits from the Prador war, including “The Skinner” and devices used for controlling slaves. He is attacked by a leech but defended by his guard spheres and carbine. A red Prador transport arrives at the home of Rebecca Frisk, a wealthy woman living under Prador rule, accompanied by Councillor Ebulan and his controlled human “blanks.” Ebulan informs Frisk of Keech’s return, causing her distress.

## Characters Present
- **Keech:** A reified (resurrected) man, possibly a former Earth Central Security monitor, investigating Spatterjay and obsessed with finding Hoop. He is equipped with a carbine, guard spheres, and an aug that seals wounds.
- **Olian Tay:** A woman with bluish skin, showing signs of the Spatterjay virus mutation. She is knowledgeable about Spatterjay’s history and trades in information.
- **Rebecca Frisk:** A wealthy woman living under Prador rule, collecting paintings and fearing Keech. She wears a human-skin jacket and is a second-class citizen of the Prador Third Kingdom.
- **Councillor Ebulan:** An old Prador who controls fifteen human blanks. He is interested in Keech’s return.
- **Ambel:** Captain of the *Treader*, experienced and pragmatic.
- **Peck:** A crew member of the *Treader*, cynical and observant.
- **Anne, Pland, Boris, Junior Crew:** Crew members of the *Treader*.
- **Jay Hoop (“Spatter”):** A man depicted in an ancient environment suit, possibly still alive and disguised as the Skinner.
- **Turbul, Hammer Whelks, Frog Whelk, Leeches, Prill, Glister:** Various creatures inhabiting Spatterjay.

## Locations
- **Spatterjay:** A planet or moon with a greenish sun, reefs, and a seabed.
- **Island:** Surrounded by ripple-shaped reefs.
- **Dingle:** A wooded area leading to Tay’s tower.
- **Clearing:** A poisoned patch of bare earth in front of Tay’s tower.
- **Stone Tower:** A building with satellite dishes, an old AGC, mirrored windows, and a conservatory.
- **Tay’s House:** Contains a luxuriously furnished room filled with books and a museum.
- **Museum:** Contains disturbing exhibits from the Prador war, including “The Skinner.”
- **The *Treader*:** A ship navigating through sargassum islands.
- **Sargassum Islands:** Areas of rotting vegetation swarming with prill.
- **Rebecca Frisk’s House/Demesne:** Contains a console, paintings, a bar, and overlooks salt flats.
- **Salt Flats:** Landscape surrounding Frisk’s house.
- **Segre Islands (aka Skinner’s Islands):** Location of a past event involving the Skinner.

## Creatures & Organisms
- **Leech:** A large creature with a glistening body, possessing a digestive tract containing a virus-neutralizing poison.
- **Turbul:** Predatory creatures.
- **Hammer Whelks & Frog Whelk:** Creatures forming part of the local food chain.
- **Prill:** Creatures resembling large lice, dangerous to Hoopers.
- **Glister:** A large, iridescent crustacean.
- **Skinner:** A creature said to strip Hoopers of their skins.

## Items & Technology
- **Scooter:** Keech’s personal transport.
- **Carbine & Guard Spheres:** Keech’s weapons and defenses.
- **Aug:** A device implanted in Keech that seals wounds.
- **Intercom & Scanning Light:** Communication and security devices at Tay’s house.
- **Satellite Dishes & AGC:** Old technology on the tower.
- **Plastiglass Cases & Holographic Displays:** Used in the museum.
- **“Full Thrall Unit” & “Spider Thrall Unit”:** Devices used to control slaves during the Prador war.
- **Bottle of Supplements:** Consumed by Tay.
- **Blunderbuss, Pump-action Shotgun, Automatic Weapon, Harpoons, Pangas, Pearwood Clubs:** Weapons used by the crew of the *Treader*.
- **Sail & Deck Cannon:** Features of the *Treader*.
- **Prador Transport:** Red, teardrop-shaped vehicle with weapon turrets.
- **Shimmer-Shield:** A defensive barrier.
- **Human-Skin Jacket:** Worn by Rebecca Frisk.
- **AG Units & Thrall Units:** Used by the Prador to control blanks.
- **Cool-ice:** A drink.

## Lore & World Details
- **Hoopers:** A group of people susceptible to the Spatterjay virus and the Skinner.
- **Spatterjay Virus:** Causes mutation (“going native”).
- **Prador War:** A past conflict involving humans and the Prador, who used “blanks” (mindless slaves).
- **Reification:** The process of being brought back to life.
- **Prador Society:** Hierarchical, with wealth indicated by the number of controlled human blanks.
- **Third Kingdom:** The Prador Third Kingdom, where Rebecca Frisk is a second-class citizen.
- **Coring:** A Prador process involving the removal and transfer of a being’s core.

## Tone & Atmosphere
The narrative blends science fiction, mystery, and a hint of body horror. The atmosphere is unsettling and tense, with a sense of danger and a long-held history of conflict and trauma. The descriptions of predatory creatures, disturbing exhibits, and the oppressive Prador presence create a feeling of unease and foreboding.
