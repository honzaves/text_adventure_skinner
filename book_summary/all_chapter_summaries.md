At the shuttle port, Keech picks up a scan-proof hexagonal container from a Golem android, Paul A2-18, keyed to Janer’s DNA. He is then ambushed by five Batians mercenaries, escaping on a hover scooter and attracting the attention of the Warden (an AI), resulting in a fine for violating flying regulations. Collapsing on a beach, Keech’s Intertox system fails, causing hallucinations of those he has killed and forcing him to consider his options: full AI conversion or using a lozenge from a lifecoven woman. He is confronted by hallucinatory figures – Frane, Cojan, and Rimsc – before realizing he is truly alone and receiving error messages about a malfunctioning cerebral probe.
