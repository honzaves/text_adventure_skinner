# Chapter: Spatterjay 1 - The Skinner

## Events
A chaotic series of events unfolds across a dangerous sea and a ruined landscape. A heirodont is attacked by a giant leech, while enforcer-drones Three and Four are destroyed during a search for an underspace signature – Three by an unknown attack, Four by a Prador war drone. Meanwhile, Roach, Keech, and Boris are ambushed by a rhinoworm while traveling on a scooter, resulting in Keech’s antiphoton weapon being lost in the water. Though Keech kills the rhinoworm, the weapon is damaged. They are then guided to shore by a massive molly carp, crashing onto a beach littered with frog whelks. Sniper, an ancient war drone, drops a replacement antiphoton weapon for Keech and engages multiple Prador drones in a fierce naval battle, sacrificing scout drone SM1 in the process. A hunting party led by Ron and Ambel pursues the source of a disembodied head, navigating a treacherous river and entering the ruins of “Hoop’s place,” where they encounter and briefly battle the monstrous Skinner. Simultaneously, Rebecca Frisk and her crew ambush Erlin, Anne, and Pland, killing Pland and taking their weapons, before abandoning the unconscious Forlam. The Warden, an observing entity, tracks Sniper’s battles, decodes a crucial quantum signal transmitted by the drone, and launches a mysterious “coffin-shape” object into the atmosphere.

- **Ron:** Captain, calm and analytical, provokes the Skinner.

- **Erlin:** Medical crewmember, cautious, questions Frisk.

## Locations
- **The Sea:** Dangerous environment with “ribbed fusion flames” and underspace signatures.
- **Historical Weapons Site:** Location where drones observed ancient flying machines.
- **The Shore/Beach:** Destination reached after the scooter crash, covered in frog whelks.
- **Shallow Water/Dingle:** Area with mounds, where the scooter travels and the carp is observed.
- **River:** Contains leeches and an onion-like creature.
- **Moat:** Filled with stagnant water and human bones, surrounding Hoop’s demesne.
- **Hoop’s Demesne:** Ruined fortress with thick walls, a courtyard, and overgrown gardens.
- **Ocean:** Area of naval battle between Sniper and Prador drones.
- **Orbital Space:** The Warden’s observation point.

## Lore & World Details
- **SM1-SM13:** Designations for different intelligences.
- **Turbul & Boxies:** Creatures with nerveless fleshy coverings.
- **Underspace Signatures:** Signals detectable in another dimension.
- **Prador:** Hostile race, possessing war drones, do not prioritize AI.
- **Hoopers:** Find death difficult to accept.
- **Batians:** Species to which Svan belongs.
- **Hoop:** Historical figure associated with the ruins.
- **Hoop’s place/Hoophold:** The ruins.

## Tone & Atmosphere
The narrative is relentlessly action-packed, gritty, and unsettling. A sense of danger and desperation permeates the story, blending high-tech weaponry with bizarre alien creatures and a bleak, unforgiving landscape. The atmosphere is chaotic and violent, with a cold, calculating undercurrent from The Warden’s observations. There's a casual acceptance of brutality and a growing sense of mystery surrounding the motivations of characters like Rebecca Frisk and the true nature of the conflict.
