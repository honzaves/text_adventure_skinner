# Chapter: Spatterjay 1 - The Skinner

## Events
The shuttle experienced a turbulent atmospheric entry, activating a safety field. While aboard, Erlin observed a reification (Keech) and Janer, a man with a large hornet on his shoulder, drawing discomfort from other passengers. Erlin joined them, learning Janer had served a two-year indenture for killing a hornet – a crime due to the insect’s connection to a Hive mind. They arrived on Spatterjay via shuttle and aircab, landing inside a geodesic dome on a partially artificial island. They met a wary Hooper pilot who questioned Janer about his hornets. Erlin explained the dangers of Spatterjay, including its unique wildlife and the Spatterjay virus, while revealing she seeks a very old Hooper named Ambel. The group then checked into a metrotel outside the Polity Dome, where Polity law doesn’t apply. Keech underwent an extensive body maintenance routine, revealing extensive cybernetic augmentation, while Erlin investigated information on reifications. Janer recounted how he killed a hornet in a ringball stadium, leading to his indenture, and expressed a desire to see beyond the Dome, a request ignored by his Hive link. Humanity had previously discovered other sentient species on Earth, including hornets, revealing that while dolphins and whales lacked true intelligence, hornets were part of a vast Hive mind communicating via pheromones and now, micro-transmitters.

## Characters Present
- **Erlin:** The protagonist, experienced and returning to Spatterjay, researching Ambel and knowledgeable about the local dangers. She has white hair, black skin, and blue eyes.
- **Janer:** A man with unruly blond hair and a Maori tiki charm, connected to a Hive mind and finished with his indenture. He carries a box of hornets and is curious about the world beyond the Dome.
- **Keech:** A reification, gaunt and grey with extensive cybernetic augmentation, including a half-helmet and metal covering much of his body. He is stoic, meticulous, and concerned with maintaining his condition.
- **Hooper Pilot:** A local, jaded and wary, cleans a skinning knife and questions Janer about the hornets.
- **Ambel:** A very old Hooper, the subject of Erlin’s search, known for his strength and unusual physiology.
- **Peck:** An 180-year-old mechanic with a bizarre encounter with a leech.

## Lore & World Details
- **Hive Minds:** Entities that can indent people.
- **Reifications:** Individuals resurrected or continuing to function after death, originating from the Cult of Anubis Arisen.
- **Spatterjay:** A place where immortality is available, but not necessarily desirable.
- **Polity:** A faction with a base on Chel.
- **Hoopers:** Long-lived, physically strong, and have a unique legal system. Their physiology is altered by “viral fibres.”
- **Skinner:** A mysterious figure.
- **Solstan Years:** Unit of time.
- **Warden:** A planetary AI.
- **Intertox:** Substance needed to prevent Hoopers from becoming more like the Skinner.
- **Viral Fibers:** Bind all life forms on the island.
- **Mutualism:** The relationship between leeches and other life forms.
- **Captains:** A specific group of Hoopers.
- **Catadaption/Ophidaption:** Extreme forms of cosmetic alteration.

## Tone & Atmosphere
The overall tone is mysterious, unsettling, and slightly gritty. There's a sense of underlying tension, a hint of danger, and a world-weary atmosphere. The descriptions of strange creatures, advanced technology, and the characters’ cryptic dialogue create a sense of unease and a complex history. The setting evokes a frontier settlement with unusual inhabitants and a yearning for the unknown.
