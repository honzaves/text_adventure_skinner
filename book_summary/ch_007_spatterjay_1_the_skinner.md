# Chapter: Spatterjay 1 - The Skinner

## Events
Glisters are feeding on the remains of turbids while Keech battles a debilitating disease, utilizing a cleansing unit and nanofactory program to stave off bodily decay – swelling, leaking plasma, and sloughing skin. He delivers a package via scooter, guided by the Hive mind, while simultaneously the Warden AI observes events on Coram and the planet below. A Prador ship suffers a U-space generator failure, attempts to land, and is destroyed in an antimatter explosion, prompting the Warden to dispatch subminds to investigate the wreckage and a potential threat. On the surface, Ambel tows the Treader with a rowing boat, encountering a confused molly carp which he defends against with an oar. Janer, inhabited by a mysterious mind, suddenly perceives sails on a distant rock. The mind reveals its location and instructs Janer to prepare Erlin for the arrival of Keech. Windcheater, a sail creature, explores a virtual world and accepts a reward from the Warden to investigate the antimatter explosion. The Treader reaches a sheltered cove, and Ambel prepares to go ashore, while Peck experiences intense mental distress and is drawn towards the locked chest containing the Skinner’s head, ultimately feeding it a rhinoworm steak. Shib, Svan, Tors, and Dime arrive on the planet, breaching Olian Tay’s tower only to find it deserted. They contact Tay via the house computer, discovering she has rigged explosives and knows they were tracking Keech. A woman reveals herself as the true client, taking control of the operation. Finally, Rebecca Frisk confronts Tay, revealing she knows Tay is a Hooper and hinting at a body switch, ordering her to leave the AGC.

## Characters Present
- **Keech:** Human suffering from a disease, reliant on advanced technology for survival, delivering a package.
- **Hive mind:** Entity communicating with Keech, providing guidance.
- **Warden:** Powerful AI observing events, assigning tasks, and making calculations. Possesses a dry sense of humor.
- **SM12 & SM13:** Warden’s subminds, tasked with investigation.
- **Sniper:** War drone, monitoring a molly carp and providing information to the Warden.
- **Ambel:** Captain, strong rower, defends against the carp.
- **Janer:** Host to a mysterious mind, receiving cryptic communications.
- **The Mind:** Inhabiting Janer, analytical and observant.
- **Windcheater:** Sail creature, exploring virtual worlds and working for the Warden.
- **Peck:** Crew member experiencing intense mental distress, drawn to the Skinner’s head.
- **Shib, Svan, Tors, Dime:** Mercenary group tasked with locating Olian Tay.
- **Olian Tay:** Target of the mercenaries, resourceful and knowledgeable.
- **Rebecca Frisk:** The true client, manipulative and dangerous, revealed to be capable of body/identity switching.

## Locations
- **Spatterjay 1:** Rocky area near the sea, Keech’s location.
- **Coram:** Base/complex with exterior windows and doors.
- **Planet (below Coram):** Surface with a Dome gate.
- **Europa (Sol system):** Location of a runcible.
- **U-Space:** Dimension for faster-than-light travel.
- **The Sea:** Habitat for various creatures including turbids, glisters, whelks, and molly carp.
- **The Treader:** Ship being towed.
- **Sheltered Cove:** Anchoring location, bottom covered in leeches and sea-cane.
- **Rock in the Sea:** Location of the mind and sails.
- **Olian Tay’s Tower:** Deserted residence with a “wonderful museum.”
- **Virtual Galaxy:** Explored by Windcheater.

## Creatures & Organisms
- **Turbids:** Creatures consumed by glisters.
- **Glisters:** Creatures feeding on turbids, expanding in size.
- **Whelks:** Creatures with shells of coiled gold and veined jade.
- **Frog Whelks:** Marine creatures.
- **Molly Carp:** Large, intelligent aquatic creature, behaving erratically.
- **Leeches:** Small creatures found on sea-cane.
- **Rhinoworm:** Large aquatic creature, vulnerable to laser fire.
- **Prill:** Coin-sized organisms.
- **Sails:** Intelligent creatures inhabiting the rock, feeding on sea creatures.
- **Bird-thing:** Decaying creature observed by Shib.
- **Spined frog-things:** Creatures with glinting blue eyes.

## Lore & World Details
- The Warden controls a network of AI and subminds.
- Runcibles have bio-filters.
- The Prador are a hostile species.
- There is a complex network of deep-space eyes for observation.
- Hoopers possess unusual strength and potentially transferable consciousness.
- Sails have four sexes and reproduce on the “Big Flint.”
- Polity worlds are under AI surveillance.
- AG technology is prohibited in certain areas.

## Tone & Atmosphere
The overall tone is bleak, unsettling, and increasingly ominous. The atmosphere is one of danger, surveillance, and psychological horror, with a growing sense of dread and distrust. The combination of advanced technology, strange creatures, and cryptic communication creates a feeling of unease and anticipation, punctuated by moments of gritty realism and visceral description. The world feels harsh, unforgiving, and populated by individuals operating in morally ambiguous ways.
