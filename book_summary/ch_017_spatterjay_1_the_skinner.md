# Chapter: Spatterjay 1 - The Skinner

## Events
A heirodont is attacked by a leech and sinks to the ocean floor, surrounded by whelks. Simultaneously, Erlin and Anne are held captive by a mercenary group led by Frisk, who intends to kill them, while Shib, Svan, and the others traverse the decaying ruins of Spatterjay – remnants of Jay Hoop’s pirate base and the Prador-Human war. SM12, a rugged drone, descends into a deep-sea trench, discovering a potentially hollow boulder that turns out to be a Prador weapons turret. Elsewhere, Vrell, a young Prador, is stranded after a failed mission and learns his father, Ebulan, plans to kill him along with other “Old Captains.”  On a Polity base, the Warden AI detects Ebulan’s arrival and initiates a full lockdown, triggering an evacuation as citizens rush to runcible gates.  Sniper, a damaged Polity agent, scavenges parts from a destroyed Prador drone to repair himself. Erlin, revealing she carries the Hooper virus, attempts to escape her restraints and is targeted by Frisk, prompting Anne to attack. A large, unknown creature then appears, casting a shadow over the group as Ambel fires a blunderbuss in warning.

## Characters Present
- **Erlin:** Captive, defiant, carries the Hooper virus, and remembers past events.
- **Anne:** Captive, attacks Frisk.
- **Frisk:** Mercenary, psychopathic, enjoys inflicting pain, and leads the group. Mentions someone named “Jay.”
- **Svan:** Mercenary, pragmatic and observant.
- **Shib:** Mercenary, initially careless, humiliated by a leech attack, but remains loyal.
- **Jay Hoop:** Pirate from a thousand years ago, established a base on Spatterjay, and gained longevity from leeches.
- **Vrell:** Young Prador, stranded and betrayed by his father.
- **Ebulan:** Prador, Vrell’s father, ruthless and prioritizing his mission.
- **Sniper:** Damaged Polity agent, self-repairing and resourceful.
- **Ambel:** Fires a blunderbuss to protect Erlin.
- **Captain Sprage:** Observant human captain.
- **Windcheater:** Analytical sail creature.
- **The Warden:** AI, analytical, decisive, prioritizes security.

## Locations
- **Spatterjay:** An island used as a base by Jay Hoop’s pirates, filled with ancient ruins.
- **Deep-sea trench:** Dark, cold, and filled with unusual deep-sea life.
- **Courtyard & Corridors:** Ancient, decaying structures on Spatterjay.
- **Dingle:** Flattened area with dense vegetation.
- **Muddy Morass/Bog:** Where Vrell hides.
- **Polity Base/Coram Base:** A fortified base with a runcible gate and defensive turrets.
- **Domes:** Protected structures used for evacuation.
- **Rocky Terrain:** Landscape with vines and sparse trees.
- **Beach:** Muddy, with dead sea creatures.
- **Atoll:** Small island with a destroyed Prador drone.

## Creatures & Organisms
- **Heirodont:** Large, herbivorous deep-sea creature covered in leeches.
- **Leech:** Parasitic creature causing longevity but also injury.
- **Whelks:** Giant, deep-sea creatures feeding on remains.
- **Jellyfish, Boxies, Glisters:** Deep-sea creatures observed by SM12.
- **Prador:** Powerful, potentially violent aliens.
- **Sail (Windcheater):** Analytical creature with a metal aug.
- **Lung Birds:** Small, easily spooked birds.

## Items & Technology
- **Ceraplast:** Woven material used for cable-cuffs.
- **Ceramal:** Composite material used in construction and armor.
- **SM12:** Rugged drone with laser and sonar.
- **Hand laser:** Weapon used by Shib and Frisk.
- **Blunderbuss:** Weapon fired by Ambel.
- **Thrall Unit:** Installed by Vrell on Drum.
- **Weapons Turrets:** Equipped with anti-photon cannons and other weaponry.
- **Personal Comps:** Used for communication.
- **Runcible Gate:** Used for evacuation.
- **Communicator:** Used by Vrell.
- **Medpack:** Contains shell patches and glue.
- **Probe:** Carried by Boris.

## Lore & World Details
- **Jay Hoop & Pirates:** Established a base on Spatterjay a thousand years ago, gaining longevity from leeches.
- **Prador-Human War:** A significant conflict that occurred on Spatterjay.
- **Leech Effect:** Biting by leeches grants longevity but causes physical changes.
- **Polity:** A civilization with advanced technology.
- **Hooper Virus:** Carried by Erlin, significant enough to be known by the mercenaries.
- **Prador Society:** Prador males are considered useless after reaching adulthood.
- **Ebulan's Plan:** To kill "Old Captains" using CTDs.

## Tone & Atmosphere
The chapter is dark, gritty, and filled with a sense of impending doom. Descriptions of decay, violence, and hidden danger create a palpable tension. There's a mix of desperation, betrayal, and methodical observation, contributing to a bleak and foreboding atmosphere. The presence of ancient ruins and hostile creatures reinforces the feeling of a dangerous and unforgiving world.
