# Chapter: Spatterjay 1 - The Skinner

## Events
The heirodont attacks a giant whelk, causing the yanwood ship to sink after a breach. Boris and Roach abandon ship, tossing sprine carboys overboard and swimming towards them, encountering electrically charged water. Beyond an invisible perimeter, Boris observes dangerous creatures, including a rhinoworm. The ship fully sinks, and they are rescued by Keech in an AG scooter, which then malfunctions and partially submerges, gaining a seahorse in its luggage compartment. Keech attempts repairs while Boris recounts the crew’s fate. Janer kills a giant leech with a carbine, protecting sleeping crewmates.

The scooter is pursued by a molly carp and rhinoworm. Keech repairs the AG motor, briefly lifting the scooter, and receives assistance from SM13, a “Submind” probe connected to the Warden. SM13 is stowed on the scooter, providing a small lift, and Roach receives an illegal weapon. Janer has a mental conversation with a “mind” about sprine crystals and a potential deal. Forlam is attacked and consumed by a tall, blue, headless creature with a leech-like mouth, prompting a firefight that forces the creature to retreat into a dingle.

Ambel assists in chasing the creature and applies a crystal to his knife. Forlam receives medical attention from Erlin, Pland, and Anne. Ron orders the crew to pack up and pursue the creature. Rebecca Frisk arrives on a ship with infected “blanks” and is informed by Vrell and Speaker that she is no longer needed, to be sent ashore with mercenaries. Svan shoots Frisk after she attempts to get closer, and they discuss escape routes, with Frisk offering to secure a ship. They land on a beach, with Svan maintaining a watchful guard.

Shib and Svan bring Frisk ashore in a rowing boat and offer her a contract: find Jay and the “Old Captains” who brought the ship ashore, relaunch the ship, and return to the Dome. The crew, led by Ambel, begins tracking them through a dense dingle, discovering a large, scarred heirodont covered in leeches. They identify a corroded metal post as a “slave post” from a former “Hoophold” – a prison of Spatterjay Hoop (the “Skinner”). They observe Spatterjay Hoop watching them from a wall.

The Warden learns the Prador fleet is heading to Skinner’s Island, likely orchestrated by Ebulan to eliminate witnesses. The Warden orders enforcer drones to intercept a ship seized by Frisk and contacts Windcheater, a sail, to warn Captain Sprage about a potential “contra-terrene device” (CTD) on the island. Windcheater researches CTDs and relays the warning, contemplating the fate of his people.

## Lore & World Details
Yanwood is a heavy, non-buoyant material. Sprine is a substance contained in carboys and potentially used to prevent transformations. The Spatterjay virus alters DNA and can cause extreme physical transformations. Spatterjay Hoop (the Skinner) was a historical figure who enslaved people for the Prador. The Prador are a species involved in political maneuvering. The Polity is a large civilization. CTDs are powerful weapons with a history of planetary destruction.

## Tone & Atmosphere
The narrative is tense, desperate, and increasingly ominous. It blends immediate danger with a growing sense of historical weight and political intrigue. The descriptions are gritty and realistic, highlighting the harsh conditions and the potential for horrific transformations. A pervasive sense of distrust and impending doom hangs over the events.
