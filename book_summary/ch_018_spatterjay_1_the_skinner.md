# Chapter: Spatterjay 1 - The Skinner

## Events
A group of mercenaries—Janer, Ambel, Ron, Keech, Frisk, Boris, Roach, and Shib—are ambushed while attempting to eliminate a transforming, leech-like creature known as a “Skinner.” The Skinner, capable of swallowing prey whole and possessing a deadly bile called “Sprine,” proves incredibly resilient. The mission quickly devolves into chaos as Frisk betrays the group, attacking Ambel and Ron before fleeing. Keech prioritizes eliminating Frisk, while Shib panics and escapes into the woods, only to be attacked by aggressive, walrus-mustached beings called “Hoopers.” Ambel and Janer eventually manage to kill the Skinner, utilizing sprine to poison its nearly detached head. Meanwhile, Keech pursues Frisk, revealing both are “Hoopers” – heavily augmented individuals – and is ultimately overpowered. On a nearby island, Ebulan attempts to launch an attack but loses control of his remotely piloted “blanks” to Pilot, a rebellious crew member who kills several and seizes control of the base. Ebulan’s ship is damaged by SMs and crashes into the sea, where Pilot continues the attack, ultimately destroying Ebulan. Simultaneously, a Prador destroyer enters the system, prompting the Warden, an AI, to deploy drones for defense. An old war drone, “Sniper,” heavily modified with Prador technology, joins the fight, directing the drones to target the destroyer’s thrusters. Sniper agrees to have his consciousness subsumed by the Warden in exchange for rescue, but the transfer proves unexpectedly complex.

## Characters Present
- **Janer:** Mercenary, skilled marksman with a laser carbine.
- **Ambel:** Mercenary, uses thrown weapons and a machete, experienced fighter.
- **Ron:** Mercenary, aggressive, uses a machete.
- **Keech:** ("The Monitor") Mercenary, uses an APW, strategic, revealed to be a Hooper.
- **Frisk:** Mercenary, betrays the group, incredibly strong, also a Hooper.
- **Boris & Roach:** Mercenaries, vanish during the initial fight.
- **Shib:** Mercenary, panics and flees, encounters Hoopers.
- **Ebulan:** Ship captain, loses control of his base to Pilot.
- **Pilot:** Rebellious crew member, Hooper, relentlessly pursues and kills Ebulan.
- **Sniper:** Ancient war drone, piloting a Prador drone, cynical and agrees to subsumption.
- **Warden:** AI, directs drones, manages subminds, and attempts to absorb Sniper’s consciousness.
- **Goss:** Mentioned by a Hooper, 322 years old.
- **Svan:** Employer, described as unforgiving.

## Locations
- **Low-tech World:** The general setting for the conflict.
- **Slope & Dingle:** Initial engagement area with the Skinner, a dense forest with orange fungi and animal tracks.
- **Woods:** Where Frisk and Shib flee.
- **Peartrunk:** A tree infested with leeches, used to suspend Shib.
- **Segre Islands:** The location of the island base and the crash site.
- **Ocean:** Where Ebulan’s ship sinks.
- **Prador Destroyer:** A heavily armoured warship.
- **Dome:** The location of the Warden’s control center.

## Creatures & Organisms
- **Skinner:** Large, transforming leech-like creature, resistant to damage, with a deadly bite.
- **Molly Carp:** Prey of the Skinner, attempts to escape from within.
- **Leeches:** Infest the sea and peartrunk, grating at armor.
- **Lung birds:** Birds that burn and fall from the sky.
- **Hoopers:** Human-like beings, some with walrus mustaches, aggressive, possibly cyborgs.
- **Prador:** A species whose technology is utilized by Sniper.
- **Rhinoworm:** Attempted to bite a dinghy.
- **Turbul-inflated glister:** Food source for the molly carp.
- **Prill:** Organisms cascading from the seaweed.

## Items & Technology
- **Laser carbine:** Used by Janer, has an “autosight” function.
- **APW:** Used by Keech, fires explosive shells and can switch to cutting mode.
- **Machete:** Used by Ron and Ambel.
- **SM (Submachine gun):** Recovered by a Hooper and used against Shib.
- **Crabskin armour:** Worn by mercenaries.
- **Hot Armour:** Worn by Svan, charred and brittle.
- **Medpack:** Contains spray to deaden pain and synthiskin.
- **Satlink position finder:** Shows Svan’s location.
- **Hexagonal box:** Contains sprine.
- **Missiles:** Used by drones and Ebulan’s ship.
- **Rail-guns:** On the Prador destroyer.
- **Particle Beams:** On the Prador destroyer.
- **Prador Drone:** Piloted by Sniper, has shields and alternate vision.
- **Blanks:** Remotely piloted bodies controlled by Ebulan and Pilot.
- **Shell Cutter:** A weapon wielded by Pilot.

## Lore & World Details
- The Hoopers are heavily augmented individuals, possibly cyborgs.
- Sprine is a poison effective against the Skinner.
- The Prador are a technologically advanced race.
- The Warden can break control codes and perform “subsumption” of consciousness.
- The setting appears to be a long-lived world with a history of conflict.
- There are worlds covered in radioactive ash.

## Tone & Atmosphere
The chapter is relentlessly violent and chaotic, filled with desperate fighting, betrayal, and grim determination. A sense of urgency and danger permeates the narrative, highlighting the harshness of the world and the ruthlessness of the characters. The atmosphere is gritty and desperate, with a growing sense of unease as the lines between organic and artificial blur and the fate of the characters becomes increasingly uncertain.
